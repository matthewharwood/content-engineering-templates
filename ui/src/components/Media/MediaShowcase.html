Object-Fit Modes - Video

Videos support all the same object-fit modes as images: cover, contain, fill, none, and scale-down.

object-fit: cover

Fills container, may crop

object-fit: contain

Entire video visible, letterbox

object-fit: fill

Stretches to fill, may distort
