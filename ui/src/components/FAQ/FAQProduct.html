How do I apply the fragrance? expand_more

For best results, apply fragrance to pulse points where your skin is warmest:

  • Wrists and inner elbows
  • Behind ears and on the neck
  • Chest and collarbone area

Avoid rubbing the fragrance into your skin, as this can break down the scent molecules. Instead, let it dry naturally.

How long will the fragrance last? expand_more

Our fragrances are formulated with high-quality oils and typically last 6-8 hours on the skin. Longevity can vary based on:

  • Your skin type (oily skin holds fragrance longer)
  • Climate and humidity
  • Application method
  • The specific fragrance concentration

Pro tip: Layer with an unscented moisturizer before application to help the fragrance last longer.

How should I store my fragrance? expand_more

To preserve your fragrance quality:

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Keep the bottle tightly sealed when not in use
  • Avoid storing in bathrooms where temperature fluctuates
  • Keep away from extreme heat or cold

When stored properly, our fragrances maintain their quality for 2-3 years.
