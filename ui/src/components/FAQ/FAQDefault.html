What is your return policy? expand_more

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unused products. Items must be in their original packaging with all tags attached. Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.

How long does shipping take? expand_more

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available and takes 1-2 business days. Free shipping is offered on orders over $65.

Do you ship internationally? expand_more

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by location but typically range from 7-14 business days. Customs fees may apply depending on your country.
